Our Mission

Project Articulate is a nonprofit dedicated towards educational accessibility for public speaking, debate, and DECA, as well as using those activities to create a charitable impact.

Our primary focus on extending our efforts to historically underserved communities, particularly those who face current barriers in these activities. With the invaluable skills involvement in these activities can bring — confidence, critical thinking, and the ability to self-articulate — accessibility for all groups of people is particularly important.

Our Structure

Free Classes

At zero charge to the participant, primarily aimed at students who would otherwise not participate in activities.

For-Charity Classes

At rates significantly lower than currently offered private classes, all profits going to charitable causes.

Our History

Project Articulate first began classes in spring 2021. Since then, we’ve:

  • Introduced 150+ new students to DECA, debate, and public speaking
  • Expanded to a seven-member teaching and organising staff
  • Raised $3000+ for charitable causes
