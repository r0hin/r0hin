Millions of children die each year.

General

There are three categories or GHOs that this can sorted into. Firstly, infant deaths which are deaths from infants. Second is under-five deaths which are deaths by children under five years of age and finally neonatal deaths which are deaths during birth due to not enough resources or capabilities to perform stable birth procedures.

High child mortality rates are very common in developing countries like India.

My Solution

Spreading Awareness

Most people do no not have the slightest clue about what's going on in these places. For my design project, I created this website meant to inform people about this issue which may cause people to take action. If we don't, more and more children will continue to die.
